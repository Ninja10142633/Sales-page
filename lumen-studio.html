Sample concept — Creative agency site

Lumen Studio — clean, editorial-style creative agency website.

This concept explores a calm, editorial feel for a design studio that wants to look premium without feeling loud. Focus on typography, spacing and a balanced grid that lets visuals breathe.

Multi-page structure Portfolio-first layout Strong use of white space
lumenstudio.design — Home Preview mode
Clean hero with short headline, subtle subcopy and an understated call-to-action, followed by a simple but strong portfolio grid.

Information architecture

The site is structured to feel effortless to navigate. Each page has a clear role, and together they build trust, show expertise and make it easy for leads to reach out.

Home

Snapshot of the studio: what they do, who they help and a direct link to featured work.

  • Short, bold hero statement
  • Selected projects with short captions
  • Subtle call-to-action to view full portfolio

Portfolio

A focused place for case studies and visuals. Less text, more clarity and context.

  • Thumbnail grid with hover states
  • Optional case study pages
  • Filtering by project type (brand, web, etc.)

About & Contact

Human side of the studio + a simple way to start a conversation.

  • Story, principles and team highlights
  • Social links and email
  • Short contact form for new projects
